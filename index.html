Projects

What I Do.

Connection through creation drives me. Whether I am making music or running Discover Electronic Tulsa, much of the intrinsic value I get from a project is derived from the personal connections made.

  • Music Production

    I produce electronic music under the name Just Hall. After years of producing on my own, I have recently had the opportunity to collaborate with local artists and I love it! Process over product is my mantra.

  • Sound Engineering

    In lieu of the resources to hire professional mixing and mastering engineers, I have learned to mix and master all of my own music. I enjoy the blend of art and science in sound engineering and look for opportunities to help DIY musicians turn their musical ideas into digital music realities. Too many DIY artists' dreams go to an early grave because of lack of access to sound engineers. I want to work to bridge that gap wherever I can.

  • Community Event Promotion

    I am not an event promoter by vocation, but I saw the need for increased exposure in the electronic music community in Tulsa. In 2018, I started Discover Electronic Tulsa as a way to increase visibility for all electronic music shows and events in Tulsa. The project is currently a FB page where I share every event I can find that fits the mission.

  • Web Design

    My interest in music and community engagement has naturally led me into web design. Future projects of interest include building portfolio websites for local musicians and developing Discover Electronic Tulsa into a more useful tool outside of Facebook's control.
